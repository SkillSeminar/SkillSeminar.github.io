Understanding and Improving Human and Robot Skills and Movements

Mission

Researchers in computational motor control, skill acquisition, and robotics all try to solve closely related problems: how to learn and execute complex movements. Through this seminar series, we aim to bring together researchers approaching this problem from different perspectives in the hope of exchanging knowledge and insights.
